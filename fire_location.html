Wildland Fire in New York State (NYS)

Wildland fire location in different counties, NYS (2008 - 2018)

Dataset contains all the wildland fire, NYS (2008 - 2018)

This plot shows the wildland fire location in different county
change the county by choosing the name in selection box (left - bottom)
- the red node means each fire location
- the bigger the size, the higher the damage range
As we can see, most of the wildland fire accidents are with complex level 4 and 5, which means
Most of the fire accidents are not very serious, this can also explain why most of the accident
do not cause any fatalities and home lost. Besides, wildland fire accident normally happend in
forest which with low population density. This is also the reason why most of the accident do not
cause any fatalities and home lost.
In additon, there are interesting relationship between duration and damage range (circle size)
normally, more damage range has long duration for each fire accident.
