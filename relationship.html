Wildland Fire in New York State (NYS)

Detail information about three different factors (ownership, NFFL fuel model and fire report method)

Dataset contains all the wildland fire, NYS (2008 - 2018)

This plot shows the wildland fire under three different scenarios
You can click selection point on the top, choose the scenario you want to explore. The default state is Ownership.
When you click the circle, you can see the pie chart describing number of fire with the cause.
Interesting findings
As we explore in the third page. In most of the scenarios, campfire, debris burning, incendiary has the highest happen frequency
special situation
However, there are some situation are very special.
When you choose Ownership, and click the other goverment. In this situation, prescribed fire cause 52 fire accidents. Also, when you click wildlife management area, the prescribed fire cause 31 fire accidents. For state forest/RF area, prescribed fire cause 36 fire accidents.

This is very interesing, because at these area, goverment and forest areas protection personnel will set fire according to regulations which can better protect the wildland ecosystem. This can explain why these area has different cause compare with other scenarios.
The color shows the aveage damage range.
The darker the color, the higher the damage range
